<p><p>Single leaf door and double leaf door are two types of interior fire doors available in the market. Single leaf doors are designed as a single panel that swings open on hinges, while double leaf doors consist of two panels that open in the middle. Single leaf doors are ideal for small spaces or where space optimization is required, while double leaf doors are commonly used in larger openings or where wider clearance is needed. Both types of doors are essential for fire safety and security in buildings.</p></p>
