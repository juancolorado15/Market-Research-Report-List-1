<p><p>Coextruded Multi-layer Film market types refer to the different structures of films produced with multiple layers of materials. Asymmetrical structure films have layers with different properties, such as barrier, adhesion, or optical properties. On the other hand, symmetrical structure films have identical layers on either side of the film. Both types cater to different packaging needs, providing a variety of benefits such as improved strength, flexibility, and barrier properties, depending on the specific requirements of the end product.</p></p>
