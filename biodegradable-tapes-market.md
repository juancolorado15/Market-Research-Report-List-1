<p><p>The biodegradable tapes market can be categorized into three types: pressure sensitive, water active, and others. Pressure sensitive tapes have adhesive properties that allow them to stick to surfaces when pressure is applied. Water active tapes, on the other hand, require moisture to activate their adhesive properties. Finally, the "others" category includes tapes that possess unique characteristics not found in pressure sensitive or water active tapes. Overall, biodegradable tapes offer environmentally-friendly alternatives to traditional tapes by breaking down naturally over time and minimizing environmental impact.</p></p>
