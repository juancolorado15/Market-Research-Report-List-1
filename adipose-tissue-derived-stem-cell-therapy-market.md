<p><p>Adipose tissue-derived stem cell therapy market includes autologous stem cells, which are derived from the patient's own adipose tissue, and allogeneic stem cells, which are sourced from a donor's adipose tissue. Autologous stem cells are preferred for their lower risk of rejection, while allogeneic stem cells are used when a patient's own cells are not suitable. Both types of stem cells offer potential for regenerative medicine and therapeutic applications in various medical conditions.</p></p>
