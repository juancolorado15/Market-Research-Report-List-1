<p><p>Rechargeable Implantable Pulse Generator (IPG) Market Types refer to the different configurations in which the device can deliver electrical stimulation to the body. Unipolar IPGs have one electrode, which delivers stimulation, while the other electrode is placed outside the body. Multipolar IPGs have multiple electrodes that can deliver stimulation, providing more precise control over the therapy. Both types of IPGs are rechargeable, allowing for longer-lasting and more effective treatment of conditions such as chronic pain or movement disorders.</p></p>
