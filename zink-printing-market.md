<p><strong>The Zink Printing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ZINK-based Paper</li><li>ZINK-based Printer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Zink printing market consists of two main types: ZINK-based paper and ZINK-based printers. ZINK-based paper refers to specially coated paper that is designed to work with ZINK printers. This paper contains cyan, yellow, and magenta dye crystals that are activated by heat. When the printer applies heat, the crystals react and create vibrant, high-quality prints without the need for ink cartridges. On the other hand, ZINK-based printers are devices that utilize ZINK technology to print directly onto ZINK paper, eliminating the need for traditional ink cartridges or toners.</p></p>
