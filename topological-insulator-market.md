<p><strong>The Topological Insulator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Weak Topological Insulator</li><li>Topological Crystal Insulator</li><li>High-order Topological Insulator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Topological insulators are categorized into three main types: Weak Topological Insulators, Topological Crystal Insulators, and High-order Topological Insulators. Weak Topological Insulators have protected surface states within the bulk band gap, while Topological Crystal Insulators exhibit non-trivial topology due to the crystal symmetry. High-order Topological Insulators possess topological surface states localized at corners, edges, or hinges of the material. These different types of topological insulators have distinct electronic properties and potential applications in various fields such as quantum computing and spintronics.</p></p>
