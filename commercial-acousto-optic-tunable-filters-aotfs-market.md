<p><p>Commercial Acousto Optic Tunable Filters (AOTFs) are utilized in various applications such as spectroscopy, telecommunications, and hyperspectral imaging. There are two main types of AOTFs in the market: Collinear AOTFs and Noncollinear AOTFs. Collinear AOTFs use a single crystal and exhibit high spectral resolution, while Noncollinear AOTFs use two crystals and provide wider spectral bandwidth. Both types offer tunable filtering capabilities based on the acousto-optic effect, allowing for precise wavelength selection and filtering in various optical systems.</p></p>
