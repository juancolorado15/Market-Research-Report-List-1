<p><strong>The Waveguide-Type Optical Display Element Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Holographic Waveguides</li><li>Reflective Waveguide</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Waveguide-Type Optical Display Elements are widely used in augmented reality and mixed reality devices. Holographic Waveguides utilize diffraction gratings to project images directly onto the viewer's eyes, providing a high-quality display experience. Reflective Waveguides, on the other hand, use mirrors to reflect light and images onto the viewer's eyes. Both types of waveguides offer compact and lightweight display solutions, making them popular choices for wearable technology such as smart glasses and headsets.</p></p>
