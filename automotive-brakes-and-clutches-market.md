<p><p>Automotive brakes and clutches are essential components in vehicles that are responsible for controlling the speed and movement of the vehicle. Brakes are used to slow down or stop the vehicle, while clutches are utilized to engage and disengage the power transmission between the engine and the wheels. </p><p>The automotive brakes and clutches market has been witnessing steady growth due to factors such as increasing vehicle production, growing adoption of electric vehicles, and advancements in vehicle technology. Additionally, the increasing focus on vehicle safety and stringent government regulations regarding vehicle safety standards have further propelled the demand for advanced braking and clutch systems.</p><p>The market growth analysis suggests that the automotive brakes and clutches market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.8% during the forecast period. This growth can be mainly attributed to the rising demand for advanced braking and clutch systems with features such as anti-lock braking systems (ABS), traction control, and electronic stability control (ESC) in vehicles.</p><p>Moreover, the increasing trend towards the adoption of electric and hybrid vehicles is also expected to contribute to market growth. Electric vehicles require regenerative braking systems, which provide energy efficiency by converting the kinetic energy of the vehicle into electrical energy during braking. This has led to the development of advanced braking systems specifically designed for electric vehicles.</p><p>Furthermore, the market is witnessing technological advancements such as the development of lightweight materials and improved friction materials for brakes and clutches. These advancements aim to enhance the performance, reliability, and efficiency of automotive brakes and clutches.</p><p>In conclusion, the automotive brakes and clutches market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period due to the increasing demand for advanced braking and clutch systems, rising adoption of electric vehicles, and technological advancements in the field.</p></p>

<p><p>The automotive brakes and clutches market consists of two main types: OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) and Aftermarket. OEM brakes and clutches are provided by the original manufacturer of the vehicle and are installed during the production process. These parts are specifically designed for the particular vehicle model and meet the original equipment specifications. On the other hand, aftermarket brakes and clutches are produced by third-party manufacturers and are available for purchase after the vehicle has been manufactured. Aftermarket parts may offer more options and variations than OEM parts, catering to different customer preferences and needs.</p></p>
