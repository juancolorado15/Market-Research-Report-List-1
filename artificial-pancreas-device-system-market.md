<p><ul><li>Control-to-Range (CTR) System</li><li>Threshold Suspended Device System</li><li>Control - to - Target (CTT) System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Artificial Pancreas Device System market includes three types of systems: Control-to-Range (CTR), Threshold Suspended Device System, and Control-to-Target (CTT). CTR system maintains blood glucose levels within a specific range by adjusting insulin delivery. Threshold Suspended Device System pauses insulin delivery temporarily when glucose levels are too low. CTT system aims to maintain blood glucose levels at a specific target by adjusting insulin as needed. These systems help individuals with diabetes manage their condition more effectively by automating insulin delivery based on their glucose levels.</p></p>
