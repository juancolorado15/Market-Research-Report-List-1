<p><p>Automotive pressure sensors are integral components in various systems of a vehicle. ABS (anti-lock braking system) pressure sensors monitor brake fluid pressure, ensuring optimal braking performance. Airbag pressure sensors detect sudden changes in pressure to deploy airbags during a collision. TPMS (tire pressure monitoring system) sensors track tire pressure for safety and fuel efficiency. Engine pressure sensors monitor vital engine parameters for performance and emissions control. HVAC (heating, ventilation, and air conditioning) pressure sensors regulate climate control. Transmission pressure sensors monitor fluid pressure for smooth gear shifts.</p></p>
