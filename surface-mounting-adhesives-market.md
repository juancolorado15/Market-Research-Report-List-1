<p><strong>The Surface Mounting Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Conductive Adhesive</li><li>Conductive Adhesive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Surface Mounting Adhesives are used in electronic manufacturing for attaching components to printed circuit boards. Non-conductive adhesives are used for components where electrical conductivity is not required, providing strong mechanical bonds. On the other hand, Conductive adhesives contain metallic fillers to provide electrical conductivity between components. Non-conductive adhesives are commonly used in consumer electronics, while conductive adhesives are preferred in applications requiring electrical connectivity, such as sensors and RFID tags. Both types play a crucial role in the surface mounting process.</p></p>
