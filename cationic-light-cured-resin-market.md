<p><p>The Cationic Light Cured Resin market includes various types of products such as Oligomers, Monomers, Photoinitiators, and Additives. Oligomers are pre-polymers that provide the backbone of the final cured resin. Monomers are reactive substances used to crosslink and solidify the resin. Photoinitiators are chemicals that initiate the curing process when exposed to light. Additives are substances added to the resin formulation to enhance certain properties like adhesion, flexibility, or durability. These components work together to create a complete light-cured resin system.</p></p>
