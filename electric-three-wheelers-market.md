<p><ul><li>Side Wheel Motors</li><li>Center Motors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric three wheelers have two main types in the market: side wheel motors and center motors. Side wheel motors are situated on the sides of the vehicle and provide power to the wheels independently. Center motors, on the other hand, are located at the center of the vehicle and distribute power to all wheels simultaneously. Both types have their own advantages and disadvantages, depending on factors such as maneuverability, stability, and energy efficiency. The choice between side wheel and center motors ultimately depends on the specific needs and preferences of the user.</p></p>
