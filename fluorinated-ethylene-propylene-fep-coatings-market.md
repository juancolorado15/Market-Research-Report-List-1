<p><p>Fluorinated Ethylene Propylene (FEP) coatings are available in two main market types: powder coating and liquid coating. Powder coating involves applying a dry powder to the surface of a substrate, which is then heated to form a smooth, durable finish. Liquid coating, on the other hand, involves applying a liquid FEP solution to the surface, which is then cured to create a protective coating. Both powder and liquid coatings offer excellent chemical and heat resistance, as well as low friction properties, making them ideal for a wide range of industrial applications.</p></p>
