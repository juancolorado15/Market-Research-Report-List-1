<p><strong>The Zero Pressure Tires Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-supporting Type</li><li>Support Ring Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Zero pressure tires are mainly categorized into two types in the market: self-supporting type and support ring type. </p><p>Self-supporting type tires have reinforced sidewalls that allow them to support the vehicle's weight even when there is no air pressure in the tire. On the other hand, support ring type tires have an internal support ring that helps the tire maintain its shape and stability in case of a pressure loss. Both types offer the advantage of being able to run even with no air pressure, reducing the risk of sudden tire failure.</p></p>
