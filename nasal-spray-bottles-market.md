<p><p>Nasal spray bottles come in two main types - spray bottles with nebulizers and spray bottles with pump systems. Spray bottles with nebulizers are designed to deliver a fine mist of medication, making it easier for the medication to reach deep into the nasal passages. On the other hand, spray bottles with pump systems have a pump mechanism that allows for controlled and precise dosing of medication. Both types of nasal spray bottles are commonly used for administering medication for allergies, congestion, and other nasal conditions.</p></p>
