<p><strong>The Total Wrist Replacement Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Total Wrist Replacement</li><li>Total Wrist Fusion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Total wrist replacement and total wrist fusion are two different treatment options for individuals with severe wrist arthritis or injury. </p><p>Total wrist replacement involves removing the damaged wrist joint and replacing it with an artificial joint made of metal and plastic components. This allows for improved range of motion and reduced pain, as the new joint mimics the natural movement of the wrist.</p><p>On the other hand, total wrist fusion involves permanently fusing the bones of the wrist together, eliminating joint movement entirely. This procedure is typically done when wrist replacement is not suitable or desired, but it does result in limited wrist motion.</p></p>
