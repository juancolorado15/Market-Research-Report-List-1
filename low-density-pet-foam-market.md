<p><p>Low-density PET foam is classified into two main types: Virgin PET and Recycled PET foam. Virgin PET foam is made from new, unused PET materials, ensuring high quality and purity. Recycled PET foam, on the other hand, is made from post-consumer PET waste, contributing to a more sustainable and environmentally friendly product. Both types of PET foam offer similar properties in terms of insulation and lightweight characteristics, but recycled PET foam helps reduce the environmental impact by promoting the reuse of materials.</p></p>
