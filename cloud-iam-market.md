<p><strong>The Cloud IAM Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Access Management</li><li>User provisioning</li><li>Single Sign-On (SSO)</li><li>Directories</li><li>Password Management</li><li>Audit governance and Compliance Management</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cloud IAM (Identity and Access Management) encompasses several market types. Access Management refers to allowing or limiting user access to resources in a cloud environment. User provisioning involves automated user account creation and configuration. Single Sign-On (SSO) allows users to authenticate once and access multiple applications. Directories serve as centralized repositories for user information. Password Management includes secure storage and management of passwords. Audit governance ensures compliance with regulations and industry standards. Compliance Management handles monitoring and reporting of security controls and policies in place to meet regulatory requirements.</p></p>
