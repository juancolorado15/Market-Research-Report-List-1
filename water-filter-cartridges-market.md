Ceramic filters cartridges use porous ceramic material to trap contaminants, while reverse osmosis membrane cartridges remove impurities by forcing water through a semi-permeable membrane. Activated carbon filter cartridges absorb pollutants through a chemical bonding process. Other types of cartridges may include UV filters or sediment filters, each designed to remove specific types of impurities from water.</p></p>
