<p><p>Varifocal eyeglass lenses come in two main types: Freely-Varifocal Lenses and Multifocal Lenses. Freely-Varifocal Lenses offer a seamless transition between different focal points without distinct lines, providing clear vision at all distances. On the other hand, Multifocal Lenses have visible lines separating different focal points for near, intermediate, and distance vision. Both types cater to individuals with presbyopia or age-related vision changes, offering convenience and improved vision quality for various everyday activities.</p></p>
