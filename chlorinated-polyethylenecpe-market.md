<p><p>Chlorinated Polyethylene (CPE) is a type of polymer that can be classified into two main market types: Thermoplastic Resin Type and Elastomer Rubber Type. The Thermoplastic Resin Type market involves CPE being used as a resin in various plastics applications, offering excellent impact resistance and weatherability. In contrast, the Elastomer Rubber Type market utilizes CPE as a rubber additive to enhance flexibility and durability in rubber products such as hoses, belts, and seals. Both market types cater to different industries looking for specific properties in their materials.</p></p>
