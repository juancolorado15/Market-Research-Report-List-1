<p><strong>The Maintenance Free Battery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorbed Glass Mat Battery</li><li>Gel Battery</li><li>Sealed VR Wet Cell Battery</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Maintenance free batteries are categorized into different types based on their technology and construction. Absorbed Glass Mat (AGM) batteries use fiberglass mats to absorb and hold the acid electrolyte, making them spill-proof and maintenance-free. Gel batteries use silica gel to immobilize the electrolyte, preventing leakage and increasing durability. Sealed VR Wet Cell batteries are valve-regulated to maintain internal pressure and prevent leakage. Other types of maintenance-free batteries include Lithium-ion and Nickel-cadmium batteries, which offer high energy density and long cycle life.</p></p>
