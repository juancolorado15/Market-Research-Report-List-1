<p><strong>The Solid Surface Countertops Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Press Molding</li><li>Casting Molding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Solid surface countertops market can be classified into two main types: Press Molding and Casting Molding. Press molding involves pressing a mixture of resin and stone particles into a mold under high pressure, resulting in a smooth and durable countertop surface. Casting molding, on the other hand, involves pouring the resin mixture into a mold and allowing it to cure naturally, creating a unique and custom countertop design. Both methods offer a wide range of design options and flexibility for different applications.</p></p>
