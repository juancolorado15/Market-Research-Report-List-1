<p><ul><li>Integrated Type</li><li>Split Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Integrated type gangways for railway vehicles are designed as a single unit that connects two cars together, providing a seamless transition for passengers and crew. Split type gangways, on the other hand, are separate units that are installed on each car and connect when the vehicles are coupled together. Both types of gangways serve the same purpose of facilitating movement between railway cars, but they differ in their design and installation methods. Each type of gangway has its advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the railway system.</p></p>
