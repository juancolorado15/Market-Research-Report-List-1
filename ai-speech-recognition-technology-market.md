<p><p>AI Speech Recognition Technology market can be broadly categorized into two types: online market and offline market. </p><p>The online market refers to speech recognition technology that operates in real-time through an internet connection. It allows users to convert their speech into text or commands online, making it convenient for various applications such as virtual assistants or voice-controlled devices.</p><p>On the other hand, the offline market involves speech recognition technology that functions locally on devices without requiring an internet connection. This type of technology is commonly used in applications where privacy concerns or unreliable internet connectivity exist, such as in-car voice recognition systems or offline transcription services.</p></p>
