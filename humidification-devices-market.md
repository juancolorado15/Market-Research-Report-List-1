<p><ul><li>Vapor Type Humidifiers</li><li>Water Spray Humidifiers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Humidification devices market consists of two main types - vapor type humidifiers and water spray humidifiers. Vapor type humidifiers work by creating steam from heated water, adding moisture to the air. They are effective for larger spaces. On the other hand, water spray humidifiers release a fine mist of water into the air, increasing humidity levels. They are suitable for smaller areas and are more cost-effective. Both types of humidifiers are essential for maintaining proper humidity levels in indoor environments for health and comfort.</p></p>
