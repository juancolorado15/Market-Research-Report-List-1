<p><strong>The Dive Buoys Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SMB</li><li>DSMB</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dive buoys come in two main types - Surface Marker Buoys (SMB) and Delayed Surface Marker Buoys (DSMB). SMBs are used by divers to indicate their presence to the surface while underwater and signal their location during ascent. DSMBs are deployed at depth and inflated with a regulator to send a signal to the surface for boat pickup or to mark a specific underwater location. Both types of buoys enhance diver safety and communication during dives.</p></p>
