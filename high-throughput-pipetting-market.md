<p><p>High-Throughput Pipetting Market includes two main types of systems: Manual Pipetting System and Electric Pipetting System. Manual Pipetting System involves manually operating pipettes to transfer and dispense liquids, suitable for laboratories with low sample volumes. Electric Pipetting System, on the other hand, utilizes automated technology to improve efficiency and accuracy in high-throughput environments where large quantities of samples need to be processed quickly. Both systems cater to different needs in the laboratory setting, offering options for various throughput requirements.</p></p>
