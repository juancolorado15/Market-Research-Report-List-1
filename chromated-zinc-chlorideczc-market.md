<p><p>Chromated Zinc Chloride (CZC) is available in two main forms in the market - Powder CZC and Solid CZC. Powder CZC is finely ground and is commonly used in various industrial applications such as metal surface treatment and wood preservatives. Solid CZC, on the other hand, is in a solid form and is typically used in applications where a slower release of the active ingredients is desired, such as in anti-corrosion coatings and wood treatments. Both forms of CZC offer effective protection against corrosion and are widely utilized in various industries.</p></p>
