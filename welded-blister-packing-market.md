<p><ul><li>High Frequency Welded Blisters</li><li>Radio Frequency (RF) Welded Blisters</li><li>Ultrasonic Welded Blisters</li><li>Thermowelded Blisters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Welded blister packing market offers different types of welding techniques including High Frequency Welded Blisters, Radio Frequency (RF) Welded Blisters, Ultrasonic Welded Blisters, and Thermowelded Blisters. High Frequency Welded Blisters use high-frequency waves to seal the blister packs, while RF Welded Blisters use radio frequency energy for welding. Ultrasonic Welded Blisters utilize ultrasonic vibrations to join materials, and Thermowelded Blisters rely on heat to weld the packaging together. Each type of welding technique offers unique benefits and is used based on specific requirements of the packaging industry.</p></p>
