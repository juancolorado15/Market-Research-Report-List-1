<p><p>Multifocal eyeglass lenses market primarily consists of three types: Bifocals, Trifocals, and Others. Bifocals have two distinct optical powers – one for near vision and one for distance vision, making them ideal for individuals with presbyopia. Trifocals, on the other hand, have three focal points – near, intermediate, and distance vision. The Other category includes progressive lenses, which provide a smooth transition between different focal points. Each type caters to specific vision needs and preferences of individuals, offering a versatile solution for vision correction.</p></p>
