Chick Embryo Cell vaccine is obtained from embryonated hen's eggs, while Human Diploid Cells vaccine is cultivated using human fetal lung cells. <p><p>The Human Rabies Vaccine market is categorized into two segments based on its application: Pre-Exposure and Post-Exposure Vaccines. Pre-Exposure Vaccine is administered to individuals at high risk of exposure to rabies, such as veterinarians or lab workers. This vaccine helps in building immunity against the virus before any potential exposure occurs. Post-Exposure Vaccine, on the other hand, is given to individuals who have already been bitten or scratched by an animal suspected of carrying rabies. It helps prevent the virus from spreading and developing into the disease.</p></p>
