<p><p>The Sodium Ion Battery Electrode Market Types include Cathode Material and Anode Material. The Cathode Material refers to the positive electrode in the battery where the sodium ions migrate during the charging and discharging process. Common materials used for cathodes include transition metal oxides and polyanionic compounds. On the other hand, the Anode Material is the negative electrode in the battery where sodium ions are stored when the battery is charged. Anode materials commonly used include carbon-based materials and high-capacity alloys.</p></p>
