<p><p>The Aerospace Industry Torque Sensor Market includes two main types of torque sensors: Rotary Torque Sensors and Non-Contacting Torque Sensors. Rotary Torque Sensors measure torque by rotating a shaft and are commonly used in applications where space is limited. Non-Contacting Torque Sensors use magnetic or optical technology to measure torque without physical contact, making them ideal for high-speed and high-precision applications. These sensors play a crucial role in ensuring accurate torque measurement and control in aerospace industry applications.</p></p>
