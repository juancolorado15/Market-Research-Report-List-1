<p><p>Gas shut-off valves come in various types, including motorised valves and solenoid valves. Motorised valves are controlled by an electric motor and are typically used in HVAC systems to regulate the flow of gas. Solenoid valves, on the other hand, are electromechanical devices that use an electric current to control the flow of gas. Both types of valves play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and efficiency of gas systems, making them essential components in the gas shut-off valve market.</p></p>
