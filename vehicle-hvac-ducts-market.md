<p><p>Vehicle HVAC ducts can be classified into two main types: gas ducts and liquid ducts. Gas ducts are typically used to distribute airflow in the HVAC system of a vehicle and are commonly made of materials like plastic or metal. On the other hand, liquid ducts are used for circulating coolant or other liquids throughout the system to regulate the temperature inside the vehicle. Both types of ducts play a crucial role in maintaining a comfortable and consistent climate within the vehicle.</p></p>
