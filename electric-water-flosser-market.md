<p><ul><li>Constant Frequency</li><li>Frequency Conversion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric water flossers are available in two main market types: constant frequency and frequency conversion. Constant frequency water flossers maintain a steady water pressure and frequency throughout use, providing consistent cleaning power. On the other hand, frequency conversion water flossers allow users to adjust the water pressure and frequency to suit their needs. Both types offer effective cleaning of teeth and gums, but users may prefer one over the other based on personal preference and dental requirements.</p></p>
