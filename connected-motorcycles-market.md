<p><strong>The Connected Motorcycles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cellular V2X</li><li>Dedicated Short Range Communication</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The connected motorcycles market includes two types of communication technologies, namely Cellular V2X (Vehicle-to-Everything) and Dedicated Short Range Communication (DSRC). Cellular V2X enables motorcycles to communicate with other vehicles and infrastructure using cellular networks, allowing them to exchange information about road conditions, traffic, and potential hazards. On the other hand, DSRC is a short-range wireless communication technology specifically designed for vehicle-to-vehicle and vehicle-to-infrastructure communication. It enables motorcycles to communicate with nearby vehicles and infrastructure within a limited range, facilitating safer and more efficient transportation on the roads.</p></p>
