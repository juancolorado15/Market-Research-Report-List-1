<p><p>Industrial valve market types include gate, globe, ball, butterfly, plug, and other specialty valves. Gate valves are used for on/off control in pipelines. Globe valves regulate flow through a stopper-like disc. Ball valves use a rotating ball to control flow. Butterfly valves use a disc rotating in a circular motion. Plug valves use a cylindrical or conical plug to control flow. Specialty valves include diaphragm, check, and pressure relief valves, among others, designed for specific applications.</p></p>
