<p><p>Automatic pipetting workstations are available in two main market types: single-channel and multi-channel automated pipetting workstations. Single-channel automated pipetting workstations are designed for dispensing liquids into individual wells or tubes one at a time, while multi-channel automated pipetting workstations have multiple channels allowing for simultaneous dispensing into multiple wells or tubes. Both types of workstations offer increased accuracy, efficiency, and consistency compared to manual pipetting, making them ideal for various laboratory applications.</p></p>
