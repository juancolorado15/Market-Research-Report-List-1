<p><p>Automatic powder fillers are divided into two main types based on their level of automation: fully automatic fillers and semi-automatic fillers. Fully automatic fillers require minimal human intervention as they can complete the entire filling process without constant supervision. On the other hand, semi-automatic fillers require operators to perform certain tasks such as placing containers or monitoring the filling process. Each type of filler offers different levels of automation and flexibility to suit the specific needs of the production process.</p></p>
