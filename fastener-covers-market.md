<p><p>Fastener covers can be classified into various types based on the material used for coating. Zinc and nickel fastener covers offer protection against corrosion and are widely used in automotive and construction industries. Silver covers provide high conductivity and are used in electrical applications. Phosphate covers offer lubricity and anti-galling properties, commonly used in manufacturing and industrial settings.</p></p>
