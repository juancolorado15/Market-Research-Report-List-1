<p><p>Coronary blood flow meters are devices used to measure blood flow in the coronary arteries. There are three main types of coronary blood flow meters: electromagnetic blood meters, ultrasonic blood flow meters, and ultrasonic Doppler blood flow meters. Electromagnetic blood meters use magnetic fields to detect blood flow, while ultrasonic blood flow meters use sound waves. Ultrasonic Doppler blood flow meters combine ultrasonic technology with Doppler effect to measure blood flow velocity. Each type of coronary blood flow meter has its own advantages and applications in clinical settings.</p></p>
