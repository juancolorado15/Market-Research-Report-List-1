<p><p>Dual emission X-ray absorptiometry (DEXA) equipment market includes two main types: Central DEXA Bone Densitometers and Peripheral DEXA Bone Densitometers. Central DEXA Bone Densitometers are used to measure bone mineral density in the hip and spine, providing accurate diagnosis and monitoring of osteoporosis. Peripheral DEXA Bone Densitometers, on the other hand, are portable devices used for screening and monitoring bone health in peripheral areas such as the wrist, heel, or finger. Both types of DEXA equipment play a crucial role in the assessment and management of bone health.</p></p>
