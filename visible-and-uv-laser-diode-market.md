<p><p>Visible and UV laser diode markets can be categorized into single mode and multi-mode types. Single mode laser diodes emit light in a single spatial mode, resulting in a narrow beam with high coherence and long range. These are suitable for applications requiring high precision such as optical communication. On the other hand, multi-mode laser diodes emit light in multiple spatial modes, producing a wider beam suitable for applications like material processing and medical treatments. Each type offers specific benefits depending on the desired application.</p></p>
