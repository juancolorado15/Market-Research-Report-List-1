<p><p>Dimmable film is available in two main types: self-adhesive and non-adhesive. On the other hand, non-adhesive dimmable film requires separate adhesives or mounting methods, offering more flexibility in terms of application and compatibility with different materials. Both types provide the ability to control light transmission and privacy levels, making them versatile options for various commercial and residential settings.</p></p>
