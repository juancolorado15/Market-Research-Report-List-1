<p><strong>The Semiconductor Photoresist Developer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Photoresist Developer</li><li>Negative Photoresist Developer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor photoresist developers can be classified into two main types: positive photoresist developers and negative photoresist developers. Positive photoresist developers are used to remove the exposed areas of a photoresist coating, leaving behind the unexposed areas. On the other hand, negative photoresist developers are used to remove the unexposed areas of a photoresist coating, leaving behind the exposed areas. Both types of developers play a crucial role in the semiconductor industry for creating intricate patterns on substrates during the lithography process.</p></p>
