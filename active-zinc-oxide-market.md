<p><p>Active Zinc Oxide is available in different market types based on their particle size. Nano-Zinc Oxide refers to particles that are extremely small, usually less than 100 nanometers in size, which offers enhanced properties such as higher surface area and reactivity. Particle Grade zinc oxide has larger particles than nano-zinc oxide but still exhibit good dispersion and reactivity. Super-fine zinc oxide has even larger particles, typically in the micron range, but still provides excellent performance in various applications. Each type caters to specific needs in industries like cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, and electronics.</p></p>
