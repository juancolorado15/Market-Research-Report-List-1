<p><p>Biliary plastic stents are tubular devices made of plastic material that are inserted into the bile duct to treat biliary obstruction. These stents help in relieving blockages in the bile duct caused by various conditions such as gallstones, tumors, or strictures. They are flexible, easy to insert, and can be easily removed once the blockage is resolved.</p><p>The Biliary Plastic Stents Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.3% during the forecast period. The growth of the market can be attributed to factors such as the increasing incidence of biliary disorders, growing geriatric population, technological advancements in stent design, and the rising demand for minimally invasive procedures. Additionally, the increasing prevalence of diseases such as pancreatitis, cholangiocarcinoma, and bile duct stones is driving the demand for biliary plastic stents.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the Biliary Plastic Stents Market is the development of fully covered biliary stents that reduce the risk of stent-related complications such as migration and tissue ingrowth. Manufacturers are focusing on introducing stents with improved design features to enhance the efficacy and safety of biliary stent placement procedures. The market is also witnessing collaborations and partnerships among key players to expand their product offerings and geographical reach.</p></p>
