<p><p>Dust suppression systems are used to control and eliminate dust particles in various industrial settings. Dry dust suppression systems utilize specialized equipment to remove dust through methods like gravitational settling or filtration. Wet dust suppression systems, on the other hand, involve spraying water or a chemical solution to weigh down dust particles and prevent them from becoming airborne. Both systems are designed to improve air quality and maintain a safe and productive working environment by minimizing the harmful effects of dust on workers' health and equipment efficiency.</p></p>
