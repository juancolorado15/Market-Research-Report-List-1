<p><p>New Energy Vehicle Insurance market includes two main types of insurance coverage: compulsory insurance and optional insurance. Compulsory insurance is required by law and typically covers third-party liability in the event of an accident. Optional insurance provides additional coverage such as collision, comprehensive, and roadside assistance. While compulsory insurance is mandatory, optional insurance allows for customizable coverage based on the vehicle owner's individual needs and preferences. Both types of insurance play important roles in providing financial protection for New Energy Vehicle owners.</p></p>
