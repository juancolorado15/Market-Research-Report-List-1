<p><p>Refrigerated truck bodies come in three main types: closed, box, and semi-mounted. Closed refrigerated truck bodies are fully enclosed, offering maximum protection and insulation for perishable goods. Box refrigerated truck bodies are similar to closed body types but feature a separate refrigeration unit. Semi-mounted refrigerated truck bodies have the refrigeration unit mounted on the front of the vehicle, offering easier access for maintenance and repair. Each type caters to different needs within the refrigerated trucking industry, providing options for various businesses and applications.</p></p>
