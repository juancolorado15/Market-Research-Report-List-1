<p><p>High Density PTFE Tapes are classified into two main types: Ordinary and High Temperature Resistance. Ordinary PTFE tapes are designed for general sealing applications at normal operating temperatures. On the other hand, High Temperature Resistance PTFE tapes are specifically formulated to withstand extreme heat conditions, making them ideal for applications where high temperatures are present. Both types of PTFE tapes offer excellent chemical resistance, low friction, and high dielectric strength, making them versatile options for sealing and insulating in various industries.</p></p>
