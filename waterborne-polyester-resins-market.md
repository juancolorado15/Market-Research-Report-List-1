<p><ul><li>Saturated Waterborne Polyester Resins</li><li>Not Saturated Waterborne Polyester Resins</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Waterborne polyester resins are used in various industries for applications such as coatings, adhesives, and composites. The market can be classified into two types: saturated waterborne polyester resins and not saturated waterborne polyester resins. Saturated waterborne polyester resins are those in which all the available sites for cross-linking or saturation have been filled, resulting in a high level of stability and durability. Not saturated waterborne polyester resins, on the other hand, have unsaturated sites that can be further cross-linked, making them more reactive and suitable for specific applications that require flexibility and adhesion.</p></p>
