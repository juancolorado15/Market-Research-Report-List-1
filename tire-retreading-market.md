<p><p>Tire retreading is a process that involves giving new treads to worn out tires to extend their lifespan. There are two main types of tire retreading market: Pre Cure and Mold Cure. Pre Cure retreading involves applying a pre-cured tread rubber to the casing using a layer of cushion gum. Mold Cure retreading involves applying raw rubber on the casing and then curing it in a mold. Both methods have their advantages and are used in different applications based on the specific needs of the tire.</p></p>
