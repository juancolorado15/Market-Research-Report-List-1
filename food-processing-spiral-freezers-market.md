<p><p>Food processing spiral freezers are divided into two main types: single drum spiral freezers and double drum spiral freezers. Single drum spiral freezers have a single rotating drum that freezes products as they travel through the spiral conveyor belt. On the other hand, double drum spiral freezers have two rotating drums, providing higher freezing capacity and efficiency. Both types of spiral freezers are commonly used in the food industry to quickly and effectively freeze various food products for preservation and storage.</p></p>
