<p><p>The Internet of Things (IoT) Data Management market comprises three main types: Public Cloud, Private Cloud, and Hybrid Cloud. The Public Cloud refers to a network infrastructure managed and operated by a third-party provider, enabling IoT data storage and processing accessible over the internet. Private Cloud, on the other hand, involves a dedicated infrastructure managed by an organization for exclusive use, ensuring higher security and control. Lastly, the Hybrid Cloud combines elements of both Public and Private Cloud, allowing IoT data to be seamlessly stored and processed across multiple platforms for enhanced flexibility and efficiency.</p></p>
