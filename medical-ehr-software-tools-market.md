<p><p>The Medical EHR Software Tools market can be categorized into two types: Cloud-based and On-premise. Cloud-based software tools are hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet, allowing healthcare providers to easily access patient records from any location. On-premise software tools, on the other hand, are installed locally on the healthcare provider's servers and require in-house management and maintenance. While cloud-based solutions offer flexibility and scalability, on-premise solutions provide greater control and security. Healthcare organizations choose between these options based on their specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
