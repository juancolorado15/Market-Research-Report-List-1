<p><p>Luminescence detectors are devices used to measure the emission of light from a sample. Tube luminescence detectors utilize a photomultiplier tube to detect emitted light, while microplate luminescence detectors are designed for high-throughput screening applications using a microplate format. Both types of detectors are commonly used in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, and environmental monitoring to analyze fluorescence, chemiluminescence, and bioluminescence reactions. These detectors play a crucial role in research and development by providing valuable insights into biological and chemical processes.</p></p>
