<p><p>The aerospace industry utilizes various types of semi-finished steel materials including rods, sheets, plates, tubes, and other forms. Rods are long, cylindrical pieces used for structural components. Sheets are flat pieces used for skin panels or bulkheads. Plates are thicker and used for harder-wearing parts. Tubes are cylindrical hollow pieces used for hydraulic systems or conduits. Other forms may include forgings or castings, which are customized shapes or components. Each type of steel material plays a crucial role in the manufacturing and assembly of aircraft components.</p></p>
