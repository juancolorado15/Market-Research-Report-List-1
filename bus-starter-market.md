<p><strong>The Bus Starter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Control</li><li>Electromagnetic Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bus starter markets can be categorized into two types: Direct Control and Electromagnetic Control. In Direct Control, the starter motor is directly connected to the bus engine, providing instant power for starting. Electromagnetic Control, on the other hand, uses an electromagnetic switch to activate the starter motor, offering a more controlled and efficient starting process. Both types of markets cater to the automotive industry, providing essential components for ensuring the reliable ignition of bus engines.</p></p>
