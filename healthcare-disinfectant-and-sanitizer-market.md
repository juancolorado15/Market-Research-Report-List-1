<p><p>The healthcare disinfectant and sanitizer market includes two main types of products: disinfectants and sanitizers. Disinfectants are substances that are used to destroy or neutralize a wide range of pathogens, such as bacteria, viruses, and fungi. They are typically used on surfaces, instruments, and in the environment to prevent the spread of infections. On the other hand, sanitizers are agents that reduce the number of microorganisms present on surfaces to a safe level, usually by at least 99.9%. They are often used on skin and objects in healthcare settings to maintain hygiene and prevent the transmission of diseases.</p></p>
