<p><strong>The TPE Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ventilate</li><li>Non-ventilate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>TPE film market can be categorized into ventilate and non-ventilate types based on their permeability to gases and moisture. Ventilate TPE films allow for the passage of gases and moisture, making them suitable for applications where breathability is important, such as in packaging for fresh produce. On the other hand, non-ventilate TPE films have low permeability, making them ideal for applications requiring moisture and gas barrier properties, such as in medical packaging or electronics. Both types cater to different market needs and have their unique advantages.</p></p>
