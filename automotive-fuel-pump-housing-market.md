<p><p>Automotive fuel pump housing can be categorized into two main types: electric fuel pump housing and mechanical fuel pump housing. </p><p>Electric fuel pump housing utilizes an electric motor to generate the necessary pressure to deliver fuel to the engine, making it more efficient and precise in its fuel delivery. </p><p>On the other hand, mechanical fuel pump housing relies on the movement of engine components, such as the camshaft or crankshaft, to create the pressure needed to deliver fuel, which can be less efficient but more reliable in some situations.</p></p>
