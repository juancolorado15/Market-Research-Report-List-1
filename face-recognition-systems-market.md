<p><strong>The Face Recognition Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>2D Face Recognition</li><li>3D Face Recognition</li><li>Thermal Face Recognition</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Face recognition systems market can be classified into three types: 2D face recognition, 3D face recognition, and thermal face recognition. </p><p>2D face recognition technology analyzes facial features based on a 2D image captured by a camera. It is widely used in various applications such as access control systems and mobile devices.</p><p>3D face recognition goes beyond 2D by capturing depth information of facial features, enabling more accurate identification and authentication. This technology is commonly employed in high-security applications like government agencies and airports.</p><p>Thermal face recognition utilizes thermal cameras to capture the heat signatures emitted by individuals' faces. It is useful in situations where there are challenges with lighting conditions or when capturing traditional visual images is not feasible, like in low-light environments or during nighttime surveillance.</p></p>
