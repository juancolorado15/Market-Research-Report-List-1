<p><p>Absorbent surgical dressings are used for managing wounds in medical settings. Primary/secondary wound dressings are directly in contact with the wound and promote healing. Secondary dressings provide additional protection and absorbent capabilities. Absorbents are materials that absorb exudate from wounds. Bandages provide support and compression. Adhesive tapes secure dressings in place. Protective dressings provide a barrier against external contaminants. Each type of dressing plays a specific role in wound care to ensure proper healing and infection prevention.</p></p>
