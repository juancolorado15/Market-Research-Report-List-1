Automation And Control System (ACS) encompasses overall automation activities. Manufacturing Execution System (MES) manages real-time production operations. Safety Instrumented Systems (SIS) ensures safety by minimizing risks. All these systems work collectively to enhance efficiency, productivity, and safety in the oil and gas sector.</p></p>
