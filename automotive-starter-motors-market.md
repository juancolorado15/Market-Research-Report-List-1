<p><strong>The Automotive Starter Motors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Control</li><li>Electromagnetic Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive starter motors are categorized into two main types- direct control and electromagnetic control. Direct control starter motors are more traditional, with a direct mechanical connection to the engine. On the other hand, electromagnetic control starter motors use electromagnetic switches to engage the motor. Direct control starters are simpler and cheaper, but electromagnetic control starters offer more precise control and are more efficient. Both types have their own advantages and are used in various applications within the automotive industry.</p></p>
