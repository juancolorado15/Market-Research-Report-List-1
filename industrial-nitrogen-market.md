<p><p>Industrial nitrogen is widely used in various industries for applications such as pressurizing pipelines, purging equipment, and creating inert atmospheres. The market for industrial nitrogen can be categorized into two main types: compressed gas and liquid nitrogen. Compressed gas nitrogen is stored in high-pressure cylinders, making it suitable for portable applications and smaller-scale uses. On the other hand, liquid nitrogen is stored in cryogenic tanks and is preferred for larger-scale operations due to its higher density and lower cost per unit volume.</p></p>
