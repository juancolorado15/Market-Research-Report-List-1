<p><strong>The Cermet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Carbide-base</li><li>Oxide-base</li><li>Nitride-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cermet materials can be classified into three main types based on their composition: Carbide-based, Oxide-based, and Nitride-based. Carbide-based cermets are composed mainly of carbides such as tungsten carbide and titanium carbide. Oxide-based cermets are made of oxides like alumina and zirconia. Nitride-based cermets consist primarily of nitrides such as silicon nitride and aluminum nitride. Each type of cermet material offers unique properties and characteristics, making them suitable for different applications in various industries.</p></p>
