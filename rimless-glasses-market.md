<p><p>Rimless glasses, as the name suggests, are eyeglasses that have no frames around the lenses. The lenses are held in place either by a thin metal or plastic bridge connecting the lenses and nose pads, or with the help of frameless drill mounts that are secured directly into the lenses. Rimless glasses provide a more minimalist and lightweight feel compared to their framed counterparts, giving wearers a subtler and more sophisticated look.</p><p>The rimless glasses market is experiencing steady growth, driven by several factors. One of the main drivers is the increasing prevalence of eye-related disorders and the subsequent rise in demand for eyewear. Rimless glasses are particularly popular among professionals and individuals seeking a sleek and modern appearance.</p><p>Additionally, advancements in lens manufacturing technology have contributed to the growth of the rimless glasses market.
