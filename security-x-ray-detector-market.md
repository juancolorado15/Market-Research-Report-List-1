<p><p>Security X-Ray Detectors are primarily classified into two main types - Cabinet X-Ray Detectors and Portable X-Ray Systems. Cabinet X-Ray Detectors are designed for fixed installations in locations like airports, government buildings, and security checkpoints. These detectors offer high throughput and accuracy for screening large volumes of items. On the other hand, Portable X-Ray Systems are compact, lightweight devices that are used for mobile security screening operations in areas where fixed installations are not practical or available, such as at events or in remote locations.</p></p>
