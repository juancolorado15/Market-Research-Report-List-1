<p><strong>The Commercial Vehicle Friction Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Brake Linings</li><li>Brake Pads</li><li>Clutch Facings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial vehicle friction material market consists of three main types: brake linings, brake pads, and clutch facings. Brake linings are used in drum brake systems to provide friction and heat resistance for stopping the vehicle. Brake pads are used in disc brake systems to provide similar functions. Clutch facings are used in clutch systems to provide friction for engaging and disengaging the transmission. Each type of friction material plays a crucial role in ensuring the safety and efficiency of commercial vehicles.</p></p>
