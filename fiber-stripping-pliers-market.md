<p><p>Fiber stripping pliers are used for removing the outer insulation from fiber optic cables before splicing or termination. Single hole pliers are designed to strip the fiber cable in one smooth motion, while three holes pliers offer a more precise and controlled stripping process. Both types are commonly used in telecommunications, data centers, and other industries where fiber optic cables are installed and maintained.</p></p>
