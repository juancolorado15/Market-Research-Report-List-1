<p><p>In the market, disc thickeners are available in two main types: continuous and batch. Continuous disc thickeners operate continuously, processing a constant flow of material without interruption. Batch disc thickeners, on the other hand, operate in batches, processing material in discrete units at a time. The choice between continuous and batch disc thickeners depends on the specific requirements of the application, such as the desired processing capacity and level of control over the thickening process.</p></p>
