<p><p>Weak Acid Cation Exchange Resin (WAC) is a type of resin commonly used in water treatment processes. The two main types of WAC resins are Gel Weak Acid Cation Exchange Resin and Macroporous Weak Acid Cation Exchange Resin. Gel WAC resins have a smaller particle size and higher surface area, making them more suitable for applications where a high exchange capacity is required. Macroporous WAC resins have a larger pore structure, allowing for faster ion exchange rates and better performance in high flow rate applications.</p></p>
