<p><p>Selective emitter solar cell market types refer to different manufacturing processes used to enhance the efficiency of solar cells. Masking and etching involves applying a mask on the cell's surface and then etching it to create selective areas for better current collection. Self-alignment involves using self-aligned passivation layers to improve overall cell performance. Self-doping relies on incorporating dopants during the cell fabrication process to create highly doped regions for improved electron transport. These techniques help to boost the solar cell's conversion efficiency and reduce costs in the production process.</p></p>
