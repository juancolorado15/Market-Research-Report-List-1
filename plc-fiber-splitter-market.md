<p><p>PLC Fiber Splitter market offers various types including 1x4, 1x8, 1x16, and Others. A 1x4 splitter divides one input signal into four output signals, while a 1x8 splitter divides it into eight outputs. A 1x16 splitter provides sixteen outputs. The "Others" category may include different configurations based on the application requirements. These different splitter types cater to various network sizes and setups, providing flexibility and scalability for different communication systems.</p></p>
