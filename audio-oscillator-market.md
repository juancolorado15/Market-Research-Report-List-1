<p><p>The audio oscillator market can be segmented into low audio frequency oscillators and high audio frequency oscillators. Low audio frequency oscillators are designed to generate signals in the frequency range audible to humans, typically up to 20,000 Hz. These are commonly used in audio equipment for tasks like testing and calibration. On the other hand, high audio frequency oscillators produce signals above the audible range, typically used in applications like ultrasonic testing and medical imaging. Both market types cater to different industries and applications based on their frequency output.</p></p>
