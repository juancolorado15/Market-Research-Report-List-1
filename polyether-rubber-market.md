<p><p>Polyether rubber market consists of three main types: Propylene Oxide Rubber, Epichlorohydrin Rubber, and Copolymerization Chlorohydrin Rubber. Propylene Oxide Rubber is known for its excellent resistance to chemicals and high flexibility. Epichlorohydrin Rubber offers good heat resistance and oil resistance, making it suitable for automotive and industrial applications. Copolymerization Chlorohydrin Rubber combines the benefits of both Propylene Oxide Rubber and Epichlorohydrin Rubber, offering a balanced mix of properties such as heat resistance, oil resistance, and flexibility. These different types cater to various industries and applications within the polyether rubber market.</p></p>
