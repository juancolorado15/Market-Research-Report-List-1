<p><ul><li>Water Atomization</li><li>Gas Atomization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Atomized brass powder is produced using two main methods: water atomization and gas atomization. Water atomization involves spraying molten brass into a water stream, resulting in fine powder particles with high purity. Gas atomization, on the other hand, involves spraying molten brass with gas to create spherical particles with controlled size distribution. Both methods offer unique characteristics and applications, with water atomization being preferred for superior purity and gas atomization for producing uniform spherical particles.</p></p>
