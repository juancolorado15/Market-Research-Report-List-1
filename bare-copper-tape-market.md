<p><p>Bare copper tape is available in three different market types: annealed (soft), half-hard, and hard. Annealed copper tape is soft and flexible, making it easy to bend and shape. Half-hard copper tape is slightly more rigid than annealed, allowing for a moderate amount of bending. Hard copper tape is the most rigid and difficult to bend, making it suitable for applications that require a more durable and stable material. Each market type offers different levels of flexibility and strength to suit a variety of needs.</p></p>
