<p><p>RF repeater systems are commonly divided into two main types: microwave RF repeaters and linear RF repeaters. Microwave RF repeater systems utilize higher frequencies to amplify and extend the range of signals, making them suitable for long-distance communication such as in satellite and mobile networks. On the other hand, linear RF repeater systems are used in scenarios where signal clarity and consistency are crucial, such as in broadcasting and public safety communications. Both types play a critical role in expanding and enhancing wireless communication networks.</p></p>
