<p><p>Additives for inkjet inks market types include humectants and co-solvents, which help maintain ink viscosity and prevent drying out. Surfactants aid in ink spreadability and adhesion to various substrates. Defoamers reduce foam formation during ink production and printing. Rheology modifiers control ink flow and stability. Other additives may include anti-oxidants, anti-microbial agents, or colorants. Each type of additive plays a specific role in improving the performance and quality of inkjet inks for various printing applications.</p></p>
