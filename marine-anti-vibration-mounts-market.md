<p><ul><li>Cylindrical Mounts</li><li>Bushing Mounts</li><li>Conical Mounts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Marine Anti-Vibration Mounts are types of mounts used to reduce noise and vibration in marine applications. Cylindrical Mounts consist of a rubber element bonded between two metal plates for damping vibrations in all directions. Bushing Mounts are made of a rubber bushing bonded to a metal sleeve, providing excellent isolation from vibration and noise. Conical Mounts are designed with a conical shape to provide high deflection and flexibility for absorbing vibration and shock in marine environments. Each type of mount offers unique benefits for specific applications in the marine industry.</p></p>
