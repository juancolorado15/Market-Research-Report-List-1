<p><p>Flexitanks are large, flexible containers used to transport non-hazardous liquids in bulk. These factors are expected to fuel the adoption of flexitanks in various industries, including food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and chemicals.</p><p>Furthermore, the growing trend of adopting environmentally-friendly packaging solutions is also contributing to the growth of the flexitanks market. Flexitanks are recyclable and produce less waste compared to other bulk liquid transportation methods, making them a more sustainable option for manufacturers and shippers.
