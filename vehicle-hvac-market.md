<p><p>The Vehicle HVAC market can be categorized into Independent Type and Not Independent Type. Independent Type refers to HVAC systems that operate separately from the vehicle's engine, providing cooling or heating even when the engine is turned off. On the other hand, Not Independent Type HVAC systems rely on the vehicle's engine to function, only providing cooling or heating when the engine is running. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and vehicle specifications in the market.</p></p>
