<p><p>Single Vision Spectacle Lenses refer to lenses that have a single prescription power throughout the entire lens and are commonly used for correcting nearsightedness, farsightedness, and astigmatism. On the other hand, Progressive Lenses are designed to provide a smooth transition of prescription powers for different distances, allowing users to see clearly at all distances without visible lines in the lens. Both types cater to different vision needs and preferences, offering solutions for various vision impairments.</p></p>
