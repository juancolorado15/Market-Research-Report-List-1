<p><p>The Biosurgery Sealants and Hemostats market consists of three major types: Hemostats, Internal Tissue Sealants, and Adhesion Barriers. Hemostats are used to stop bleeding during surgeries or traumatic injuries. Internal Tissue Sealants are designed to seal internal tissues and prevent any leakage or fistula formation after surgeries. Adhesion Barriers are used to prevent the formation of scar tissues or adhesions between organs post-surgery. These products serve as essential tools in various surgical procedures, promoting efficient and effective patient outcomes.</p></p>
