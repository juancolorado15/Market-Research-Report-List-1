<p><p>The Civil Defense UAVs market is categorized into three types: Rotary Airfoil, Fixed-Wing, and Others. Rotary Airfoil UAVs are equipped with rotary blades that enable vertical take-off and landing, making them suitable for close-range surveillance and reconnaissance missions. Fixed-Wing UAVs, on the other hand, have fixed wings that allow for longer flight endurance and higher altitudes, making them ideal for long-range missions such as border patrolling. The Others category includes hybrid designs and specialized UAVs for specific tasks within civil defense operations.</p></p>
