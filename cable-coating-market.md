<p><strong>The Cable Coating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>IntumescentCableCoatings</li><li>Non-IntumescentCableCoatings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Intumescent Cable Coatings are designed to expand and form a protective char layer when exposed to fire, providing insulation and protecting the cable from damage. Non-Intumescent Cable Coatings, on the other hand, do not expand when exposed to heat but instead provide a thin layer of protection against fire. Both types of coatings are used in the cable industry to enhance the fire resistance and safety of cables in various applications.</p></p>
