<p><p>The cough syrup market can be categorized into three types: cough suppressants, expectorants, and antihistamines. Cough suppressants are designed to suppress the cough reflex, providing temporary relief by blocking the brain's cough center. Expectorants promote the expulsion of mucus from the respiratory tract, thus helping to alleviate chest congestion. Antihistamines are primarily used to treat allergies and cold symptoms, as they reduce the production of histamines in the body, which can cause sneezing, runny nose, and coughing. These different types cater to various symptoms and provide targeted relief for individuals suffering from respiratory issues.</p></p>
