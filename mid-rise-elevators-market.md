<p><p>Mid-rise elevators are categorized into two main types: freight elevators and passenger elevators. They are equipped with larger platforms and higher weight capacities compared to passenger elevators. Passenger elevators, on the other hand, are designed to transport people between different floors in a mid-rise building. They generally have more sophisticated features such as touch screen controls, sleek interiors, and advanced safety systems to ensure a smooth and safe ride for passengers.</p></p>
