<p><p>Vapor Recovery Units (VRU) are used to capture and recover vapors released during various industrial processes to comply with environmental regulations. There are three main types of VRU markets: Condensation, Adsorption with Carbon Beds or Molecular Sieve, and Absorption. Condensation units cool the vapor to condense it into liquid form. Adsorption units use carbon beds or molecular sieves to trap vapors. Absorption units use liquids to absorb and recover the vapors. Each type of VRU market offers unique advantages depending on the specific application and requirements.</p></p>
