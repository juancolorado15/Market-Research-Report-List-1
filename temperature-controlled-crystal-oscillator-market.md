<p><p>Temperature Controlled Crystal Oscillator market consists of various types based on the output signal generated: Clipped Sine TCXOs provide a square wave output, suitable for digital applications. CMOS TCXOs feature low power consumption and are popular in battery-powered devices. HCMOS TCXOs combine the advantages of CMOS with high amplitude output. Sine TCXOs produce a pure sine wave output, ideal for precision applications. Other types include LVCMOS and LVPECL TCXOs which offer specific voltage level outputs for various applications in industries like telecommunications and aerospace.</p></p>
