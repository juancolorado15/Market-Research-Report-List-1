<p><strong>The Bench-top Automated Immunoassay Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chemiluminescence Analysis</li><li>Fluorescent Immunoassay</li><li>Radioimmunoassay</li><li>Enzyme Immunoassay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bench-top automated immunoassay analyzer market is divided into four types: chemiluminescence analysis, fluorescent immunoassay, radioimmunoassay, and enzyme immunoassay. Chemiluminescence analysis employs a chemical reaction to generate light, enabling the detection of specific antibodies or antigens. In fluorescent immunoassay, fluorescent tags are used to detect the presence of specific compounds. Radioimmunoassay involves the use of radioactive isotopes to measure the concentration of antigens or antibodies. Lastly, enzyme immunoassay utilizes enzymes to produce a detectable signal, indicating the presence of specific analytes.</p></p>
