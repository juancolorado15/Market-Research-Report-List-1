<p><strong>The Freight Forwarding Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full Container Load (FCL)</li><li>Less-than Container Load (LCL)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Freight Forwarding Services market consists of various types, including Full Container Load (FCL), Less-than Container Load (LCL), and Others. FCL involves shipping a full container of goods owned by a single shipper. LCL, on the other hand, combines goods from multiple shippers into a single container to fill it completely. This option is suitable for small shipments that don't require a whole container. The "Others" category encompasses specialized services like temperature-controlled shipping, oversized cargo, or freight consolidation. These options cater to different shipping needs and provide flexibility and efficiency in global cargo transportation.</p></p>
