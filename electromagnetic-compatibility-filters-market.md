<p><p>Electromagnetic Compatibility Filters (EMC) are categorized into two main types: Type I and Type II. Type I filters are designed for applications where the filter will be connected directly to the power mains. They are typically used in industrial environments to suppress conducted electromagnetic interference. Type II filters, on the other hand, are designed for applications where the filter will be connected between the power mains and the equipment. They are commonly used in consumer electronics to reduce conducted and radiated interference.</p></p>
