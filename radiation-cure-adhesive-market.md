<p><ul><li>UV Curable Adhesives</li><li>Electron Beam Curable Adhesives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radiation Cure Adhesives are adhesive products that are cured using radiation sources such as UV light or electron beams. UV Curable Adhesives are cured when exposed to ultraviolet light, which triggers a chemical reaction that causes the adhesive to harden. Electron Beam Curable Adhesives, on the other hand, are cured using high-energy electron beams. Both types of adhesives offer fast curing times, superior bond strength, and resistance to heat and chemicals, making them ideal for a wide range of industrial applications.</p></p>
