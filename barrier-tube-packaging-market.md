<p><p>Barrier tube packaging is available in various types of materials including plastic (LDPE, HDPE, PP, PA, EVOH) and metal (aluminum, tin). Plastic tubes are commonly used for their flexibility and ability to maintain product freshness. LDPE and HDPE offer excellent resistance to moisture and chemicals, while PP is known for its high heat resistance. PA and EVOH provide exceptional oxygen barrier properties. Metal tubes such as aluminum and tin are preferred for their durability and ability to block out light and oxygen, thus preserving the product for a longer period.</p></p>
