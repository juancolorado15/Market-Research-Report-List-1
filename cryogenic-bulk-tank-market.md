<p><p>Cryogenic bulk tanks are used for storing and transporting liquefied gases at extremely low temperatures. Two main types of cryogenic bulk tanks are vertical tanks and horizontal tanks. </p><p>Vertical tanks are designed to stand upright and are more space-efficient for large-volume storage applications. Horizontal tanks, on the other hand, are laid horizontally and are better suited for situations where space is limited or where transportation requirements necessitate a lower profile tank design. Both tank types play a critical role in various industries requiring cryogenic storage solutions.</p></p>
