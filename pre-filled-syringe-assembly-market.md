<p><p>Pre-filled syringe assemblies are available in various market types such as COP (Cyclic Olefin Polymer), COC (Cyclic Olefin Copolymer), PP (Polypropylene), and Others. COP and COC are known for their superior chemical resistance and low protein adsorption, making them ideal for sensitive drug formulations. PP is a common choice for cost-effective solutions with good chemical resistance. The "Others" category includes materials like glass and PET, offering different advantages based on specific application requirements and compatibility with drug formulations.</p></p>
