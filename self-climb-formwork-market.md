<p><p>Self climb formwork market types include Electric Jack Climbing Formwork and Hydraulic Jack Climbing Formwork. Electric Jack Climbing Formwork uses electric jacks to lift the formwork to the desired height, providing efficiency and ease of use. On the other hand, Hydraulic Jack Climbing Formwork utilizes hydraulic power for lifting operations, ensuring precise and controlled movements. Both types offer a safe and reliable solution for constructing tall structures by allowing the formwork to climb independently without the need for external equipment.</p></p>
