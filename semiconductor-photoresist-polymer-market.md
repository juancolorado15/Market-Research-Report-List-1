<p><p>Semiconductor photoresist polymers are classified into various types based on their chemical composition and properties. DNQ-Novolac photoresist, which is widely used in the industry, offers high resolution and sensitivity. Epoxy-based polymers provide good adhesion and durability. Off-stoichiometry Thiol-enes (OSTE) polymers offer versatility and excellent pattern transfer capabilities. Other types of photoresist polymers are also available in the market, each offering unique features and cater to specific needs in semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
