<p><strong>The Ship Loading/Unloading Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Ship Loading/Unloading Systems</li><li>Pneumatic Ship Loading/Unloading Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mechanical Ship Loading/Unloading Systems involve the use of conveyor belts, bucket elevators, and grab buckets to load and unload cargo from ships. These systems are commonly used for handling bulk materials such as coal, grains, and ores. </p><p>Pneumatic Ship Loading/Unloading Systems utilize compressed air to move bulk materials into or out of ships through pipelines or hoses. This system is faster and more efficient than mechanical systems, making it ideal for handling materials that are prone to degradation or dusting.</p></p>
