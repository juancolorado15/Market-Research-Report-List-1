<p><ul><li>Tubeless Wheel Rims</li><li>Tube Wheel Rims</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vehicle wheel rim market is classified into two main types - Tubeless Wheel Rims and Tube Wheel Rims. Tubeless Wheel Rims are designed to provide airtight seal with the tire, eliminating the need for inner tubes. These rims are popular for their lightweight and fuel efficiency benefits. On the other hand, Tube Wheel Rims require inner tubes to hold the air pressure. They are known for their durability and ability to sustain high loads. Both types serve specific purposes based on the vehicle's design and requirements.</p></p>
