<p><strong>The Programmable Application Specific Integrated Circuit (ASIC) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full-custom Design</li><li>Gate-array Based</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Programmable ASIC market includes full-custom design, gate-array based, and others. Full-custom design involves designing every aspect of the ASIC from scratch, providing maximum flexibility but also requiring extensive time and resources. Gate-array based ASICs offer pre-defined logic cells that can be interconnected by the user, providing a balance of flexibility and efficiency. Other types of programmable ASICs may include semi-custom approaches or newer technologies such as field-programmable gate arrays (FPGAs). Each type offers different levels of customization and trade-offs in terms of cost and performance.</p></p>
