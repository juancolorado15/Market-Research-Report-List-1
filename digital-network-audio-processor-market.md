<p><p>Digital Network Audio Processor Market includes two main types: Dante Standard and Livewire Standard. Dante Standard is a widely used audio networking technology that allows distribution of high-quality audio over IP networks. Livewire Standard is a proprietary audio-over-IP protocol developed by Livewire. Both types offer reliable and efficient solutions for transmitting audio signals over networks, catering to the needs of various industries such as broadcast, live sound, and commercial installations. These technologies have become essential tools for audio professionals seeking seamless audio distribution.</p></p>
