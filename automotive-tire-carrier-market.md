<p><p>Bumper Mounted Tire Carriers are designed to securely hold a spare tire on the front or rear bumper of a vehicle. This type of tire carrier is typically bolted onto the bumper and provides easy access to the spare tire without taking up space in the cargo area. </p><p>Tailgate Reinforcement Style Tire Carriers attach directly to the vehicle's tailgate, reinforcing its strength to support the weight of a spare tire. This type of carrier is convenient for easy access to the spare tire and can help prevent damage to the tailgate from the added weight.</p></p>
