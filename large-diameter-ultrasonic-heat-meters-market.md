<p><p>Large Diameter Ultrasonic Heat Meters come in two main types - Integrated Heat Meters and Split Heat Meters. Integrated Heat Meters combine the flow and temperature sensors into a single unit, making them easy to install and maintain. Split Heat Meters have separate flow and temperature sensors connected by a cable, allowing for more flexibility in installation. Both types of meters are used in larger diameter pipes for accurate measurement of heat energy consumption in commercial and industrial applications.</p></p>
