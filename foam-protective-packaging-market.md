<p><p>Foam protective packaging is categorized into different types based on the materials used. Expanded Polystyrene (EPS) is a widely adopted foam material due to its lightweight and shock-absorbing properties. Polyurethane foam provides excellent cushioning and is often used for fragile or delicate items. Expanded Polyethylene (EPE) offers good flexibility and impact resistance. Expanded Polypropylene (EPP) has high durability and is commonly used for heavy-duty applications. Other types of foam packaging materials may include biodegradable or recyclable options, as well as specialty foams tailored for specific protective needs in the packaging industry.</p></p>
