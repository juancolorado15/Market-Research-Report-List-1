<p><p>Hardboard panels are commonly categorized into three main types in the market: MDF (medium-density fiberboard), plywood, and others. MDF panels are made from wood fibers and resin, offering a smooth surface for painting or veneering. Plywood panels consist of several thin layers of wood glued together, providing strength and stability. Other types of hardboard panels may include particleboard, OSB (oriented strand board), or chipboard. Each type has its own unique properties and applications, catering to different needs in the construction and furniture industries.</p></p>
