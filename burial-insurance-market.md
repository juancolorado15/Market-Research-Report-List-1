<p><strong>The Burial Insurance Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Level Death Benefit</li><li>Modified or Graded Death Benefit</li><li>Guaranteed Acceptance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Burial insurance, also known as final expense or funeral insurance, comes in different market types. Level death benefit coverage provides a full payment to the beneficiary upon the policyholder's death. Modified or graded death benefit policies have a waiting period, typically 2 or 3 years, before the full benefit is paid out. During this period, only a portion of the benefit or premiums may be refunded. Guaranteed acceptance market refers to policies that do not require medical underwriting, making them available to individuals who may have difficulty obtaining coverage elsewhere.</p></p>
